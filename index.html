Some common questions
were often asked

What exactly does the NGE device do?

The NGE device uses advanced technology to help blind individuals perceive the world around them, making it possible for them to see again by converting visual information into a sensory format they can understand.

How does the NGE device work?

The NGE device integrates real-time visual data processing with feedback mechanisms, allowing users to "see" objects, people, and their surroundings through vibrations and sound, providing a more intuitive understanding of their environment.

Is the NGE device suitable for all types of blindness?

While the NGE device is designed to help those with certain types of blindness, its suitability may vary depending on the severity and cause of vision loss. We recommend consulting with a specialist for personalized advice.

How long does it take to learn to use the NGE device ?

Most users adapt to the NGE device within a few weeks of consistent use. Our customer support team offers training sessions to help users learn how to utilize the device to its full potential.

Can the NGE device be used in everyday activities?

Yes, the NGE device is intended to be integrated into daily activities, from navigating the home to participating in outdoor environments, allowing users to engage more fully with their surroundings.

Support after purchase for NGE device?

We offer ongoing customer support, including software updates, training resources, and a 2-year warranty to ensure that users can make the most of their NGE device throughout its lifespan.
